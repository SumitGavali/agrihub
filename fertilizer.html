Brief Information regarding fertilizers

Indian agriculture has long been the backbone of the country's economy, supporting millions of farmers and providing food security for the nation. To meet the growing demand for food and sustain agricultural productivity, chemical fertilizers have played a crucial role. These fertilizers supply essential nutrients to the soil, ensuring that crops grow healthily and yield optimally.

Urea

Used for: Rice (paddy), Wheat, Sugarcane, Cotton, Maize (corn)

Content: Contains 46% nitrogen

Price: ₹285/kg

Ammonium Sulfate

Used for: Rice, Groundnut (peanuts), Cotton, Soybean, Vegetables

Content: 21% nitrogen and 24% sulfur

Price: ₹300 - ₹350/kg

Calcium Ammonium Nitrate (CAN)

Used for: Cereals (wheat, barley), Rice, Sugarcane, Vegetables (tomatoes, onions, cabbage), Tobacco

Content: 26% nitrogen

Price: ₹310/kg

Single Super Phosphate (SSP)

Used for: Wheat, Rice, Groundnut (peanuts), Cotton, Fruits (citrus, mango, apple)

Content: 16 - 20% phosphorus

Price: ₹180/kg

Diammonium Phosphate (DAP)

Used for: Wheat, Rice, Chili (pepper), Potatoes, Cotton

Content: 46% phosphorus and 18% nitrogen

Price: ₹1,200/50kg bag

Triple Super Phosphate (TSP)

Used for: Cereals (wheat, maize), Sugarcane, Fruits (citrus, mango, guava), Leguminous crops (peas, beans)

Content: 44 - 48% phosphorus

Price: ₹350/kg

Muriate of Potash (MOP)

Used for: Rice, Sugarcane, Tomatoes, Bananas, Citrus (orange, lemon)

Content: 60 - 62% potassium

Price: ₹450/kg

Potassium Sulfate (SOP)

Used for: Tomatoes, Rice, Fruits (apple, citrus), Cabbage & Cauliflower

Content: 18% sulfur and 50% potassium

Price: ₹500/kg

Potassium Nitrate

Used for: Tomatoes, Fruits (grapes, citrus), Vegetables (lettuce, carrots), Potatoes

Content: 13% nitrogen and 44% potassium

Price: ₹550/kg

NPK Fertilizers

Used for: Various crops (depending on the NPK ratio)

Content: Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K)

Price: ₹25-₹50 per kg (depending on the ratio)

NP and PK Fertilizers

Used for: Crops needing specific nutrients (Nitrogen + Phosphorus or Phosphorus + Potassium)

Content: Nitrogen + Phosphorus (NP) or Phosphorus + Potassium (PK)

Price: ₹30-₹60 per kg

Zinc Sulfate

Used for: Rice, Wheat, Maize

Content: Zinc

Price: ₹20-₹30 per kg

Ferrous Sulfate

Used for: Crops with iron deficiency (e.g., vegetables)

Content: Iron

Price: ₹25-₹40 per kg

Boron Fertilizers

Used for: Cotton, Sugarcane, Vegetables

Content: Boron

Price: ₹50-₹70 per kg
